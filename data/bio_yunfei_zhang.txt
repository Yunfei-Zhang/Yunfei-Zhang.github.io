Yunfei Zhang is a PhD student at the Chair of Traffic Engineering and Control, Technical University of Munich (TUM), supervised by Prof. Dr.-Ing. Klaus Bogenberger. 
His research interests include Autonomous Vehicles, Traffic data analysis, and Mobility-on-Demand services. 
Before joining the group, he received his bachelor in Civil Engineering from Tongji University and master in Transportation Systems also from TUM.
In his spare time, he loves all kinds of sports including CrossFit, weight lifting, tennis, ice skating, etc.
