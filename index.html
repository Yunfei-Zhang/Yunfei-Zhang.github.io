Yunfei Zhang

I am a PhD student at the Chair of Traffic Engineering and Control at the Technical University of Munich (TUM).

Previously, I finished my bachelor in civil engineering from Tongji University in China and my master in Transportation Systems also from TUM.

My research interests include Autonomous Vehicles, Traffic flow theory, and Mobility-on-Demand systems.
